THE FALL OF THE BACKSTREET BOYS

In 2005, one member of the Backstreet Boys decided he needed to leave the band as he felt burnt out and wanted to live his personal life.

That member was one of their lead singers, Kevin Richardson, but the rest of the members decided that they would not shut down the band.

They were constantly asked if they were going to replace Kevin with other popular former band members like Ryan Gosling and Justin Timberlake.

Even though they kept the band alive and constantly produced music, the numbers the Backstreet Boys were achieving were not the same.
