THE RETURN OF THE BACKSTREET BOYS

During a performace at the O2 Arena in London in 2012, the Backstreet Boys revealed they were recording a new album to the entire turnup.

To a rave of cheers and applause, they also exclaimed that Kevin Richardson was officially returning to the band after 6 years away.

The album following Kevin's return, titled "In a World Like This" debuted in the US charts at number 5, showing how much the fans had been anticipating Kevin's return.

The return of Kevin proved vital in helping Backstreet Boys return to being one of the best bands in the world, and the numbers proved that.
