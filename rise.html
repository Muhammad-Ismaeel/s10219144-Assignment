THE RISE OF THE BACKSTREET BOYS

The Backstreet Boys rose to fame with their hit debut album released in 1996 with a simple title 'Backstreet Boys'

The album became one of the biggest hit albums of all time at that point, putting the 5 men on the map.

Their next few albums performed similar numbers, with their most iconic song "I Want It That Way" being released.
